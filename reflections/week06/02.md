## Questions

1. What are props?

Props are how we pass variables and information between different components.

2. What are props used for?

Props are used to share information between different components. 

3. Where can props be used or accessed?

Props can be accessed from the parent component and can be used on any child. The only thing is that a parent can't use a prop from a child.
